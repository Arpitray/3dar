🎯 AR App - QR Code

Scan this QR code with your phone camera to access the AR experience

📱 How to Use:

  1. ⚠️ IMPORTANT FIRST: Create a .mind marker file
    • Go to: MindAR Compiler
    • Upload the image you want to scan (business card, QR, etc.)
    • Download the .mind file
    • Save it as public/targets/card.mind
    • Restart your dev server
  2. Print or display this QR code
  3. Open your phone's camera app
  4. Point at this QR code
  5. Tap the notification popup to open the URL
  6. 🎯 App launches directly in AR mode!
  7. Point your camera at the marker image you used in step 1
  8. Magic! The 3D cube appears! ✨
⏰ Note: Your ngrok URL is temporary. When you restart ngrok, you'll get a new URL and need to generate a new QR code.

💡 Tip: For permanent setup, deploy to Vercel, Netlify, or your own HTTPS server to get a permanent URL.
